This Privacy Policy explains how Oxygen Students Paradise ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our services — including printing, binding, scanning, DTP, and government application assistance — accessed via WhatsApp, our website, or in-store.

1. Information We Collect

  • WhatsApp number — used to receive your files, send quotes, payment links, and job-ready notifications.
  • Files you send — documents, images, or other files submitted for printing. Stored temporarily and deleted after job completion.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your print and service orders

3. WhatsApp Messaging

By messaging our WhatsApp number (+91 94957 06405), you consent to receive automated replies from our order management system. We use the Meta WhatsApp Business Cloud API. Your messages are also subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the services needed to operate:

Print files are retained for up to 7 days after job completion, then deleted. Job records (without file content) are kept for up to 1 year for billing and dispute resolution.

6. Your Rights
