# Printosky — Customer Growth Plan
_Last updated: 2026-04-29_
_Aligned with: [EXPANSION_PLAN.md](EXPANSION_PLAN.md)_

---

## Context

- **Current base:** Students + walk-in customers, Thrissur
- **Order channel:** WhatsApp-first (send file → quote → pay → pickup)
- **Core insight:** Kerala has 500+ colleges, highest WhatsApp penetration in India, most literate state. Students are the primary print customer — high frequency, recurring, predictable academic calendar. This is a captive market we can own before any competitor builds a digital presence.
- **Academic calendar drives demand** (plan content + capacity around this):
  - Oct–Nov: B.Tech / M.Tech thesis submissions
  - Feb–Mar: MBA / MCA / BCA project reports
  - Apr–May: All-stream final submissions + exam printouts
  - Jun–Jul: New semester notes and assignments

---

## Phase A — Own Thrissur, Signal the Markets (Month 1–12)

### Channel 1: Google My Business *(free, do today, highest ROI)*

Every "print shop near me" search in Thrissur must show Printosky first.

**Actions (Week 1):**
- Claim and fully complete GMB profile
- Add all services explicitly: Printout, Photocopy, Binding, Lamination, Spiral Binding, Thesis Printing, Project Report, ID Card, Scan, Colour Print
- Upload 10+ photos: shop front, printed thesis, binding machine, finished work
- Add WhatsApp click-to-chat as primary contact
- Respond to every review within 24 hours

**Target keywords to own:**
```
"print shop Thrissur"
"xerox shop near me Thrissur"
"thesis printing Thrissur"
"project report binding Thrissur"
"spiral binding Thrissur"
"colour printout Thrissur"
```

**Review growth target:** 50 reviews in 90 days → ask every completed customer via WhatsApp bot post-delivery message.

---

### Channel 2: WhatsApp Click-to-Chat *(zero cost, compounding)*

The order channel IS WhatsApp — remove every barrier to starting a conversation.

**Branded link:** `wa.me/91XXXXXXXXXX?text=Hi,+I+need+a+printout`

**Distribution:**
- A5 QR cards → college notice boards, canteen tables, hostel corridors, bus stop near colleges
- QR sticker on every completed order packet
- Share in college Facebook groups and Instagram comment sections
- Hostel wardens for bulk referrals (they coordinate student orders)

**Per-market UTM tracking:** `?text=From+COLLEGE_NAME` → tracks which group/college drives orders

---

---

### Channel 4: College WhatsApp Group Strategy *(highest reach, lowest cost)*

One share in the right group = 50–200 new customers overnight.

**Playbook:**
1. Identify 10 colleges within 15 km of Thrissur shop
2. For each college: recruit 1 student ambassador — offer ₹500 Amazon voucher + ₹20/referral
3. Ambassador shares click-to-chat link in department + hostel WhatsApp groups
4. Track via college-specific UTM links
5. Pay only on conversion — zero upfront CAC risk

**Target colleges (Thrissur):**
- Government Engineering College Thrissur
- Vimala College, St. Thomas College
- Christ College Irinjalakuda
- Vidya Academy of Science & Technology
- KSRTC / government offices (B2B walk-in)

---

### Channel 5: Referral Program *(turn customers into sales)*

**Mechanics:**
- WhatsApp bot sends after order completion: *"Liked our service? Share this link — you get ₹20 off your next order for every friend who orders."*
- Referral code embedded in WhatsApp link
- Pay only on conversion
- Target CAC: ₹20 (vs ₹150–400 for paid ads)

## Phase B — Franchise Visibility (Month 12–24)

### Each franchise shop gets its own local presence
- GMB setup is part of franchise onboarding checklist
- Central brand kit: logo, WhatsApp greeting message, review request template, QR card design
- Shared Instagram with shop-specific location tags
- `{shopname}.printosky.com` landing page auto-generated on onboarding

### B2B Academic Partnerships *(highest LTV channel)*

**Target:** College library departments, placement cells, project coordinators.

**Pitch:** *"We handle all final year students' thesis and project report printing. Bulk pricing. 24-hour turnaround. Invoice to department. Students don't need to travel."*

**Outreach timing:** Email department coordinators in September (before thesis season starts).
**Trial offer:** First 10 theses free.
**Revenue:** One institutional contract = ₹50,000–2,00,000/year per college.

### WhatsApp Broadcast to Existing Customers *(₹0 cost)*
- Monthly broadcast: new service, seasonal offer, or useful tip
- Segmented: students get academic tips, B2B get invoice/bulk pricing
- Meta Cloud API includes 1,000 free service messages/month
- Festival offers: Onam, Christmas, Vishu — relevant to Kerala base

### Phase B Targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Franchise shops live | 20 |
| Institutional college contracts | 5 |
| Monthly orders across network | 2,000 |
| Landing page organic traffic | 5,000/month |
| Monthly recurring SaaS revenue | ₹1,00,000 |

## Phase C — Marketplace Acquisition (Month 24+)

*Only spend on paid acquisition after supply side (shops) is proven in B.*

### Performance Marketing
- Meta Ads: college students within 5 km of active shops, 18–26 age, interests: college, university, study
- Google Search: "print shop near me" + city terms
- Budget: ₹50,000/city/month at launch, reduce as organic grows
- Target CAC: <₹150 (profitable at ₹500 average order)

### Campus Ambassador Network
- 2 ambassadors per college (hostel-level coverage)
- Monthly: ₹500 stipend + 5% commission on referred orders
- Target scale: 200 colleges × 2 = 400 ambassadors

### PR
- Yourstory / Inc42 feature: *"How Printosky prints 10,000 theses a year in Kerala"*
- Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) visibility — grants available for tech startups
- College fest sponsorships: logo on printed fest materials

### Phase C Targets (Year 1)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Customer app installs | 50,000 |
| Active cities | 4 |
| Monthly GMV | ₹1 Cr |
| CAC | <₹200 |
| NPS | >50 |

## What NOT to spend on during Phase A

| Temptation | Why to skip |
|-----------|-------------|
| Google / Meta paid ads | CAC too high before retention is proven |
| Influencer marketing | Wrong audience for print services |
| Print pamphlets / flyers | No tracking, low ROI, no digital signal |
| Native mobile app | PWA is enough; native = Phase C budget |
| LinkedIn ads | B2C print is not a LinkedIn purchase |
