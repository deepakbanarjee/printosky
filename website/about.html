Our Story

We are Printosky.

A print services brand built for speed, simplicity and quality — right here in Thriprayar, Thrissur.

Who We Are

Built to make printing effortless.

Printosky is a print services brand built on the infrastructure of Oxygen Students Paradise, based in Thriprayar, Thrissur, Kerala.

We started because we saw how much time people waste trying to get simple things printed — hunting for a shop, explaining requirements, showing up only to find it's not ready. We wanted to fix that.

So we built a simple system: upload online, get it done right, pick it up or have it delivered. No stress. No surprises.

Behind the scenes, we run high-speed production printers, binding machines, lamination equipment and a network of trusted local partners — so we can handle anything from a single CV to a bulk order of thousands of pages.

We're just getting started. Thriprayar first, then everywhere.
